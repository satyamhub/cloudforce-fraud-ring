Cloudforce Fraud Ring Demo

Detect dense UPI/device/IP sharing cliques in seconds via TigerGraph REST++. This page is styled for your round‑2 presentation and documents how your proxy + viewer work together to prove the concept.

Why it matters

Shared phones, devices, or IPs are high‑confidence signals for mule rings. RingDetect returns the accounts tied to the same objects plus built‑in risk scoring so analysts can triage faster.

How it works

Requests go through a lightweight proxy (Flask + Gunicorn) that injects a TigerGraph auth token and proxies to your Savanna REST++ instance, keeping your secrets server‑side.
